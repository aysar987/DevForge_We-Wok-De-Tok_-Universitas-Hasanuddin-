About Phishing Links

Phishing Links - is a Phishing method using malicious URL designed to trick you into revealing sensitive information or downloading malware.

Suspicious or Misspelled URLs

Legit domains are rarely misspelled. Be cautious of URLs like paypaI.com or g00gle.net. A single letter can be the difference between safe and scam.

Unfamiliar Domain Extensions

Be cautious with weird domain endings like .xyz, .top, or .click. They’re cheap and often used in phishing.
